Instructional Illustrations

Duration

1 Week

Categories

Graphic Design

A series of illustrations created over a week to instruct viewers about how to do various different tasks. The focus of the project was on simplifying complex visuals and bringing clarity to the final designs so that the images alone could provide sufficient information to the viewers about how to physically perform certain tasks.
